Tomatelli Recipe

Tomatelli is a delicious dish that is made from tomatoes and pasta. It is a great dish to make for a quick and easy dinner. Here is how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pasta
  • 2 cups of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the cherry tomatoes and garlic to the skillet and cook until the tomatoes start to burst, about 5 minutes.
  4. Once the tomatoes have burst, add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to combine.
  5. Remove the skillet from the heat and stir in the fresh basil.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
