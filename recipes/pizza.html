Pizza Recipe

Pizza is a delicious dish that is made from dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. It is a great dish to make for a quick and easy dinner. Here is how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of pizza dough
  • 1 cup of tomato sauce
  • 2 cups of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese
  • Various toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, peppers, etc.)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese over the tomato sauce.
  6. Add your desired toppings to the pizza.
  7. Sprinkle the parmesan cheese over the toppings.
  8. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  9. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
