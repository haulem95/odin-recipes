Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious dish that is made from layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and bechamel sauce. It is a great dish to make for a special occasion or a family dinner. Here is how you can make it:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 2 cups of milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. While the noodles are cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the ground beef, onion, and garlic to the skillet and cook until the beef is browned and the onion is soft.
  5. Add the marinara sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  7. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1 minute.
  8. Gradually whisk in the milk and cook until the sauce is thickened.
  9. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  11. Layer with lasagna noodles, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and parmesan cheese.
  12. Repeat the layers until all of the ingredients are used up, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  13. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  15. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
  16. Serve hot and enjoy!
